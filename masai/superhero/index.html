THOR - GOD OF THUNDER

Profile

Thor was hot-tempered and unpredictable in the same way as Herakles. Thor was also known for his incredible appetite for food and drink. He was the strongest of the gods and served as their protector and defender. Mortal people also invoked Thor for protection from evil and destruction.

Skills

  • Super human strength and speed
  • Creat energy blast
  • Quick thinking and problem-solving abilities
  • Leadership and teamwork skills
  • Kind-hearted and compassionate
  • Strong sense of justice
  • Excellent communication skills

Experience

Role: King of Asgard
Duration: Ongoing

Weapon

Strombreaker

Stormbreaker was designed by the dwarves of Nidavellir to be the greatest weapon in Asgard's history, designed for their king and capable of summoning the Bifrost Bridge on its own. However, it was never forged, and the dwarves had been rendered all but extinct.
